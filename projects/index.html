TWOWSimulator

TWOWSimulator is a project that attempts to simulate TWOW as fast and accurate as possible, and it's open-source! The github hasn't been updated in quite a while, though.

Here it is anyway!

Logbot

Logbot is my first ever Discord bot. It has a very specific purpose: to log messages from one person in one channel to another channel. It was made to form a new way to present results in TWOWs.

I'd like to thank hanss314 for helping with development (porting to discord.py rewrite + other fixes), and hosting the bot for me on his VPS!

Add it to your server here!

Project Season 2

Project Season 2 is my second Discord bot. Its main purpose is to read "scripts" generated by TWOWSimulator. However, I also included a command to tell it to send one of hundreds of computer-generated TWOW responses. They were generated using a neural network called torch-rnn, similar to what carykh used in one of his videos.

Unfortunately, I am no longer actively supporting and hosting this bot.

FMK

FMK is my third, and most goofy bot. Its only function is to play the popular game FMK, or "F**k, Marry, Kill". It simply picks random TWOWers out of a list, and makes an image with the three of them together. The art it uses is provided by JPomz.

Unfortunately, I am no longer actively supporting and hosting this bot.
